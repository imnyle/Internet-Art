Yeule

Yeule is a musical project by singaporean singer-songwriter Nat Cmiel.The earlier years of the project prominently center’s Yeules identity as it relates to the internet as a place where thaT can experiment with gender and self. For Yeule, technology was a form of building personas and engaging in escapism.


"it is on the Internet that our confrontations with technology as it collides with our sense of human identity are fresh and even raw. In the real-time communities of cyberspace, we are dwellers on the threshold between the real and virtual, unsure off our footing, inventing ourselves as we go along.”

-Sherry Turkle, Life on the Screen








“The idea of personas have always been really freeing for me
because you don’t get trapped in one body or one identity.“
