The turn to the internet and personas for many queer people comes from the queerphobia and isolation many experience in the waking world. But perhaps a rejection of the human body isn’t the answer. Artist 3, Noah Holtegaard, is a trans-masculine nonbinary Danish artist who focuses on an embrace of fundamental aspects of human culture and using technology to make them queer centric. His work explores technology as a medium for building utopia and folklore, creating the history and optimism queer people have been robbed of.
